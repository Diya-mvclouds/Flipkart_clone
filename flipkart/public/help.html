Shipping Returns & Cancellation FAQ

Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver your products safely and on time.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 4-6 business days. Express delivery (if available) takes 1-2 business days.

What are the shipping charges?

We offer FREE shipping on all orders above ₹499. For orders below this amount, a flat fee of ₹40 is applied.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be available soon.

Cancellation & Returns

How do I return a product?

You can initiate a return within 7 days of delivery. Go to "My Orders", select the item, and click "Return".

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it is shipped. Once shipped, you will have to reject the delivery or initiate a return.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Login" button on the top right, then select "Sign Up". Fill in your details to create an account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your email to receive a reset link. (Note: This is a demo feature).

Is my payment information safe?

We do not store your credit/debit card details. All transactions are encrypted and processed via secure payment gateways.
